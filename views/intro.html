Virus Invasion

Will you survive the virus ?

x

The main goal is to survive the virus by keeping your immune system high !

First game

Guess if the statements made are true or false. A good answer boosts your immune system and a bad one damages it ! (Good news: you can't die at this part)

Second game

From now, you can die, bacteria are all around you ! Move the nose with right and left arrow keys to avoid bacteria and catch the antidote to boost your immune system

Third game (if you're still alive)

Each second you lose 2% of your immune system. Catch the bacteria to have a chance to survive !
